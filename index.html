Creamy White Sauce Pasta

A quick,delicious pasta recipe
Ingrediants
  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Salt,pepeer
Instruction
  1. Boil pasta until soft.
  2. Prepare white sauce with milk and butter.
  3. Add cheese and seasoning.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce, cook for 2 mins.
  5. Add Salt and pepeer
Tips
  • Use fresh herbs for extra flavor.
  • Add veggies for a healthier version.
